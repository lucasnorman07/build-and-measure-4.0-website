Privacy Notice


Build and Measure (BAM)


Last Updated: February 12, 2026


1. Introduction

This Privacy Notice explains how Build and Measure ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the website https://buildandmeasure.app (the "Website") and the Build and Measure application (the "App").


2. The Application

The Build and Measure app does not collect or share any personal data, and does not use any tracking or analytics tools. All data for the app remains on the device unless the user decides to export and share it.


3. The Website

The Website does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We use Simple Analytics to collect anonymous website usage statistics. This helps us understand general traffic patterns and improve the Website.

Simple Analytics collects limited, non-identifying information such as:

  • Page views
  • Referrer source (e.g., search engine or direct visit)
  • Browser type
  • Country (based on anonymized IP data)

Simple Analytics does not:

  • Use cookies
  • Track users across websites
  • Collect personal identifiers
  • Store full IP addresses

For more information, see: www.simpleanalytics.com.


4. Third-Party Links

The website may contain links to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.
